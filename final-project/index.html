Welcome to Mourne

This website acts as a campaign log and [eventually, maybe] a very incomplete knowledge base for our home D&D campaign entitled "Deathless" in the homebrew world of Mourne. It contains notes and summaries written mostly by one of the party members, Lucy (with a solid assist from another party member, Nemah). Hopefully it proves to be useful and/or entertaining as the saga of The Undying Lights continues!
